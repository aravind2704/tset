Ponni Masala

பாரம்பரிய சுவை, பாட்டியின் கை வண்ணத்தில்!

Welcome to Ponni Masala, a culinary endeavour rooted in humble beginnings and deep-seated traditions. Founded in 1955 as a humble homegrown venture nestled in the picturesque village of Unjanoor, within the Sangagiri Taluk of Salem District, Ponni Foods has metamorphosed into a paragon of culinary excellence, driven by the vision of its founder, Ponni, the daughter of Vellaiyan and Kannamal.

From its inception, Ponni Masala has been committed to preserving and celebrating the rich flavours of South Indian cuisine. What began as a modest homegrown enterprise has blossomed into a distinguished business rooted in authenticity and quality. Over the years, we have curated a repertoire of 24 types of spices, each meticulously selected and blended to perfection.

At Ponni Masala, we understand the importance of staying true to our roots. Our legacy is not just a business; it's a journey that harmonizes modern efficiency with time-honoured practices. As we continue to grow, our commitment to delivering authentic, premium spices remains unwavering. Join us on this flavourful expedition and savour the taste of tradition with Ponni Masala!

Thoppur Munusami Memorial Charitable Educational Trust



Objectives:
Education: The trust runs the "KMR Spensys Innovation Foundation," serving as a student incubation centre. It facilitates access to resources and facilities that State and Central governments provide to nurture students into entrepreneurs and scientists.

Social Impact: The trust focuses on supporting socially disadvantaged but talented students studying in the college. It aids them in developing products, monetizing their creations, and raising awareness about their endeavours.

Other Initiatives: The trust engages in various activities encompassing medical aspects, skill development, innovative product development, and fostering creative thinking.

1. Target Area: Chengalpattu District, Madhurandhagam taluka.

4. Approx Cost per child: 15,000 INR
